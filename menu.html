Menu

Tacos are prepared by hand and assembled in a basket. Hot oil is then put on top of the taco basket which allows buttery finish.

  • Beans (vegan) -Pinto beans ground in smooth paste
  • Nopal (vegan) -Cactus, mushroom, corn and onion guisado makes this a hearty vegan taco
  • Potato (vegan/vegetarian)-potato a la mexicana with spices
  • Chicharron (pork marinated in spices, chile ancho)
  • Picadillo (ground turkey marinated in tomato and spices)
